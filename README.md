# no-ice-sao

Why "#NOICE"?

The #NOICE SAO is designed to remind everyone the fatal injustices of DHS and ICE enforcement in 2025–26. We cannot forget the medical neglect in concrete cells to to violent and lethal raids on our communities, ICE consistently treats immigrant lives as expendable.

This badge honors the memory of Mr. Lunas Campos, Renee Nicole Good, Alex Pretti, Luis Gustavo Núñez Caceres, Luis Beltran Yanez Cruz, Parady La, Heber Sánchez Domínguez, Victor Manuel Díaz and others.

A portion of all proceeds goes directly to the Immigrant Defenders Law Center (https://www.immdef.org/), fueling the legal defense necessary to keep families together and protect community members from deportation.

THE HARDWARE

The "#NOICE" Simple Add-On (SAO) is designed for DEFCON 34.

High-Impact Visuals: 16x side-emitting XL-1606SURC red LEDs configured to shine through the FR4 PCB.
Persistent Memory: Saves your most recently used effect in case of power loss, host battery depletion or disconnection from the host badge or totem.
Smart Power: Wide power input (2.7V-3.7V) to keep LEDs bright even when running off the DC34 badge (which only outputs 3V).

Control: One tactile button for easy effect cycling.

FEATURES & ANIMATIONS

LED Effects: Several fast and slow flashing patterns, heartbeat, breathing/fade.
